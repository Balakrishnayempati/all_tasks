Ghantasala Sai Srinivas Sivakumar (born on 16 November 1983),[3] professionally credited as Thaman S, is an Indian composer and playback singer known for his works majorly in Telugu and Tamil cinema.[4][5] He is the recipient of the National Film Award for Best Music Direction for his work in the 2020 musical hit Ala Vaikuntapuramulo.
